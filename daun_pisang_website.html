About Us

Makmur Alam Ekspor is a small-scale export business based in Medan, Indonesia, dedicated to delivering high-quality, sun-dried banana leaves. We work directly with local farmers and ensure environmentally sustainable sourcing and processing. Our leaves are ideal for craft use, packaging, decor, and more.
